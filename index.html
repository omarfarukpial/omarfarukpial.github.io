Coin Toss

Now you can toss coin how many times you want. This website is for understanding the topic of probabilty of getting head and tail in coin toss. Higher the number of toss, better the probabilty you will get. You can click the "Toss" button multiple time for same value, that will give different result. Happy tossing!
Please give a positive integer number. [Range is 1 to 1000000]
Start from small number like 10, then gradually increase the number to 100, 1000 or more.
